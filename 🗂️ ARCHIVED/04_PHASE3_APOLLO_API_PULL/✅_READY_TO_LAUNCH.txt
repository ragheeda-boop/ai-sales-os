🛑 TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Still getting 400 errors?
A: Check your Notion Integration Token in .env
   Make sure it's a NOTION token, not an APOLLO key

Q: No contacts appearing?
A: Check logs/apollo_sync.log for details
   Look for: "Created: X" or error messages

Q: Contacts appear but with empty fields?
A: Check Apollo data quality
   Some contacts may have missing email/title/phone
   These are skipped (as designed)

Q: How to stop the sync?
A: Press Ctrl+C in terminal
