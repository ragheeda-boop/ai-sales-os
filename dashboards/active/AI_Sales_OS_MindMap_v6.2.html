Lead Scoring v1.5
SCORING FORMULA
Score = (Intent × 10%) + (Engagement × 10%) + (Company Size × 35%) + (Seniority × 30%) + (Industry Fit × 15%)
Sort Score = (Lead Score × 100) + Recency Bonus (0-100)
HOT
Score ≥ 80
Urgent Call — Call Today
SLA: 24 hours
WARM
Score ≥ 50
Follow-up Email
SLA: 48 hours
COLD
Score < 50
Monitor Only
No action required

Company-Centric Operating Model (v5.0)
PRINCIPLE
Company = Primary Entity
ONE task per company per tier. ONE active opportunity per company. All automation operates at the company level.
OWNERSHIP
Apollo-First Priority (v5.1)
Apollo Account owner_id wins directly. Fallback: owner with most contacts (tie-break: most recent activity).
STATE MACHINE
Company Stage
Prospect → Outreach → Engaged → Meeting → Opportunity → Customer → Churned → Archived. Terminal stages never overwritten.
GUARD
Stage Conflict Prevention
STAGE_PRIORITY lattice + STAGE_TERMINAL set. 4 writers (sync, meeting, opportunity, governor) all guarded.
