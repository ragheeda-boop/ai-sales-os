Are HOT leads actually converting?

2
Where is revenue coming from?
100% Outbound-Apollo sourced. Financial Services (57%) and Insurance (29%) dominate by deal value. 71% of opportunities were HOT at creation time. Top role: C-Suite at 68% of meeting contacts. Avg deal: SAR 171K.

Where is the pipeline leaking?
