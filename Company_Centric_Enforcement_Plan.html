System Enforcement Plan

The current AI Sales OS implementation has 4 critical non-negotiable rules that are broken across all 5 databases. This plan identifies the gaps, quantifies the violations, and provides a step-by-step enforcement roadmap.

Current State: Task Theater (auto-created, auto-completed, zero human owners). Meeting pipeline disconnected. Opportunities DB empty.

🔍 التشخيص | Diagnosis: 4 Non-Negotiable Rules

These rules are foundational to a working company-centric sales system. Every violation breaks the enforcement chain.

The 4 Non-Negotiable Rules

Rule Definition Severity Current Violations
Rule 1: No Owner = No Record Every record (Company, Contact, Task, Meeting, Opportunity) MUST have exactly one primary owner. Records without owners are invalid. CRITICAL ~4,900+ records (Tasks 100%, Opportunities N/A)
Rule 2: Company = Primary Entity Company is the root entity. All contacts, tasks, meetings, opportunities flow through the company. No orphans allowed. CRITICAL Partially enforced: Contacts linked (good). Meetings, Opportunities disconnected (bad).
Rule 3: Company Owner = Master Owner Primary Company Owner cascades down to all company-linked records. Task Owner, Meeting Owner, Opportunity Owner derive from Primary Company Owner. CRITICAL ~14,750 companies with no Primary Company Owner. ~4,900 tasks with no Task Owner.
Rule 4: One Flow Only Company → Contact → Task/Meeting → Opportunity → Revenue. Every transition must be enforced. No dead ends. CRITICAL Chain broken: Tasks created (100% no owner). Meetings logged (0 linked to companies). Opportunities DB empty.

⚡ طبقة التنفيذ | Company-Centric 7-Step Execution Layer

The enforced workflow that every record must follow from entry to revenue.

Step 1: Company Enters System (with owner)

Trigger: Apollo.io company sync. First contact from company is added to Notion.

Action: daily_sync.py creates Companies record. Derives Primary Company Owner from contact owner (who has most contacts).

Validation Gate: Company must have at least one contact with an owner_id. No orphan companies.

Result: Company record exists with Primary Company Owner = [Ibrahim | Ragheed | Soha]

Step 2: Contacts Linked (inherit company ownership)

Trigger: Apollo.io contact sync (daily or backfill).

Action: daily_sync.py syncs contact. Links to company via relation. Writes Contact Owner as select field.

Validation Gates:

  • Contact must have owner_id from Apollo
  • Contact must have at least one non-failed email campaign (already outreached)
  • Company must exist in Notion (no orphans)

Result: Contact record exists with Company relation + Contact Owner select field populated.

Step 3: Lead Scored → Action Ready

Trigger: Contact added or updated (daily pipeline).

Action: lead_score.py calculates Score (0-100). action_ready_updater.py evaluates 5 conditions (Score >= 50, no DNC, valid outreach, valid stage, has contact method). Writes Lead Tier (HOT/WARM/COLD) and Action Ready checkbox.

Validation Gate: Action Ready = true ONLY if all 5 conditions pass. No exceptions.

Result: Contact has Lead Score, Lead Tier, Action Ready = true/false

Step 4: Task Created (Task Owner = Primary Company Owner, MANDATORY)

Trigger: Contact reaches Action Ready = true.

Action: auto_tasks.py groups contacts by company. Creates ONE task per company per tier. Task Owner = Primary Company Owner (cascaded from company). Status = "Not Started".

Validation Gates:

  • BLOCK if Primary Company Owner is NULL
  • BLOCK if company already has open task of same type (no duplicate tasks)
  • REQUIRE Company link (relation)
  • REQUIRE Task Owner select field (not null)

Result: Task exists with Task Owner, Status = "Not Started", linked to Company.

Step 5: Meeting Logged (Meeting Owner = Primary Company Owner, MANDATORY)

Trigger: Sales rep logs meeting in Notion Meetings DB (manually or from Google Calendar sync).

Action: meeting_tracker.py links meeting to company (via relation or auto-resolve from primary contact). meeting_analyzer.py analyzes meeting notes (Claude AI). Writes Meeting Owner from Primary Company Owner. Updates Company Stage = "Meeting" if outcome is positive.

Validation Gates:

  • REQUIRE Company link (explicit or resolved from contact)
  • BLOCK if Primary Company Owner is NULL
  • REQUIRE Meeting Owner select field (not null)

Result: Meeting record exists with Meeting Owner, Company link, Meeting Notes analyzed.

Step 6: Opportunity Created (from positive meeting, Company-level)

Trigger: Meeting logged with Outcome = "Stage Advance" (positive signal).

Action: opportunity_manager.py creates opportunity. Linked to company. Opportunity Owner = Primary Company Owner. Stage = "Discovery" (initial).

Validation Gates:

  • REQUIRE Company link
  • BLOCK if Primary Company Owner is NULL
  • REQUIRE Opportunity Owner select field (not null)
  • ENFORCE one active opportunity per company max. If company already has active opp, close previous before creating new.

Result: Opportunity record exists with Opportunity Owner, Company link, Deal Value, Stage = "Discovery".

Step 7: Revenue Tracked (close the loop)

Trigger: Opportunity progresses through stages (Discovery → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost).

Action: opportunity_manager.py tracks stage changes. Calculates deal value, probability, expected close. Generates revenue pipeline report grouped by Opportunity Owner (primary company owner).

Result: Revenue dashboard shows pipeline by owner. Closed Won deals attributed to owner. Feedback loop closes: Action → Meeting → Opportunity → Revenue.

Enforcement Decision Tree

Every record creation follows this decision tree:

Decision Logic ┌─ Company exists? │ └─ NO → BLOCK. Cannot proceed without company. │ ├─ Primary Company Owner exists? │ └─ NO → BLOCK. Cannot proceed without master owner. │ ├─ Company Stage is valid? │ └─ NO → BLOCK. Company must be in system (not Archived). │ ├─ Cascading Owner correct? │ └─ Task Owner = Primary Company Owner? YES → Proceed │ └─ Meeting Owner = Primary Company Owner? YES → Proceed │ └─ Opportunity Owner = Primary Company Owner? YES → Proceed │ ├─ Company Link enforced? │ └─ Task.Company linked? YES → Proceed │ └─ Meeting.Company linked? YES → Proceed │ └─ Opportunity.Company linked? YES → Proceed │ └─ Duplicate check passed? └─ No existing open task of same type for same company? YES → Create └─ No existing active opportunity for company (if creating new)? YES → Create If ALL checks pass → Record created with MANDATORY owner field If ANY check fails → Record BLOCKED, error logged, stats incremented
